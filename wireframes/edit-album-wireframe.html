Recommended Shape

Use a full-cover sheet. Editing an album can change cover, name, lock state, and password, so the user benefits from a focused screen with clear Cancel and Done actions.

A smaller sheet is better for quick one-field edits, like renaming only. For this flow, a medium sheet would feel cramped once the keyboard, cover menu, and password field appear.

The main redesign move is to make the cover the visual anchor, group the fields into one quiet rounded section, and keep destructive actions separated at the bottom.
